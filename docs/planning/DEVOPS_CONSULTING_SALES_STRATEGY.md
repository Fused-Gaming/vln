- **Buying Triggers:**
  - About to launch testnet/mainnet

  - Frustrated with manual deployments
  - Just raised funding

**Blockchain Gaming Companies**
- Similar profile but focused on gaming infrastructure
- Often need more complex multi-environment setups
- Higher budget tolerance ($2K-10K)
- Longer sales cycle but better retention

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

#### "We'll just hire a DevOps engineer"
**Response:** "That's a great long-term plan! But hiring takes 3-6 months. We can get you set up in 24 hours, and your new DevOps engineer will thank you for the head start. Many of our clients use us as a bridge while they hire."

#### "Can't we just use templates/boilerplates?"
**Response:** "Absolutely, templates are a start. But they're generic and often outdated. We customize for YOUR stack, YOUR workflows, and YOUR security requirements. Plus, we provide 30 days of support to ensure everything works perfectly."

#### "This seems expensive for a one-day project"
**Response:** "Consider the alternative: Your lead developer spending 2-3 weeks figuring this out (at $100-150/hour = $16K-36K in opportunity cost), with suboptimal results. Or hiring a DevOps consultant at $150-200/hour (easily $10K+ for similar work). We're the fast, affordable option."

#### "We're not ready yet / Still in development"
**Response:** "Actually, that's the BEST time! Setting up CI/CD early means you never waste time on manual deployments. Every commit gets tested automatically. Every push is ready to deploy. Your velocity will 10x."

#### "We're too early-stage for this"
**Response:** "That's exactly why you need this. Early-stage is when bad DevOps habits form. Starting with solid automation means you'll never have to 'fix' it later. Plus, investors love seeing proper CI/CD infrastructure."
