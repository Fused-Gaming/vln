## ğŸ¯ Key Success Factors

### Do's âœ…
- **Respond fast** - Within 2 hours during business hours
- **Personalize outreach** - Reference recent company news
- **Follow up consistently** - 3 touches minimum
- **Deliver on time** - 24-48 hours as promised
- **Over-communicate** - Daily updates during delivery
- **Request feedback** - After every project

### Don'ts âŒ
- **Don't spam** - Quality over quantity in outreach
- **Don't over-promise** - Under-promise, over-deliver
- **Don't skip documentation** - Critical for client success
- **Don't forget follow-up** - Set reminders for every action
- **Don't neglect existing clients** - Retention > acquisition
