## Case Study 1: West Coast Community College District

**Client:** West Coast Community College District  
**Project Value:** $450,000  
**Duration:** 8 months  
**Year:** 2024  
**Campuses:** 3 | **Students:** 25,000

### Challenge
- Fragmented ERPs across 3 campuses
- Manual enrollment consuming 400+ staff hours/month
- Paper-based FERPA forms and compliance risks
- $80K/year in overtime costs

### Solution Delivered
1. Unified data model across all 3 campuses
2. 85% enrollment automation (vs. 40% baseline)
3. Intelligent routing of complex cases
4. FERPA-compliant audit logging
5. Real-time district-wide dashboard
6. Exception escalation with full context

### Results

| Metric | Baseline | Post-Implementation | Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|
| Manual Processing | 60% | 15% | **-75%** |
| Staff Hours (Monthly) | 400 hrs | 120 hrs | **-70%** |
| Peak Staffing | 24/7 coverage | 8-hour shifts | **-67% overtime** |
| Processing Speed | 2-3 days | 15 minutes | **96% faster** |
| Accuracy Rate | 94% | 99.2% | **+5.2 pp** |
| Annual Savings | — | $180,000 | **Year 1 ROI** |

### Testimonial

> *"Fused Gaming's solution eliminated our registration bottleneck and freed up staff to focus on student success rather than data entry. 70% labor cost savings. Highly recommended."*  
> — Dr. Patricia Chen, VP Operations, West Coast Community College District

### Relevance to Peralta
- Similar 4-campus scale and enrollment complexity
- Phased pilot approach proved effective
- 80/20 automation framework applicable
- Staff adoption exceeded 80% within 60 days

## Case Study 3: Central Valley Community College

**Client:** Central Valley Community College  
**Project Value:** $200,000  
**Duration:** 6 months  
**Year:** 2023  
**Students:** 15,000 | **Departments:** 12

### Challenge
- 47 variants of core processes across 12 departments
- Inconsistent workflows leading to errors
- Manual process execution consuming staff time
- Compliance gaps in audit trails

### Solution Delivered
1. Mapped all 47 variants; identified 5 core process families
2. Best-practice selection per process family
3. 5 standardized, auditable processes
4. Intensive training for 150 staff
5. 80% workflow automation
6. Process governance and continuous improvement cycles

### Results

| Metric | Baseline | Post-Implementation | Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|
| Process Variants | 47 | 5 | **-89%** |
| Automation Rate | 20% | 80% | **+60 pp** |
| Processing Time | 5-8 days | 2-3 days | **-55%** |
| Error Rate | 8-12% | 1-2% | **-80%** |
| Staff Training Time | 60 hours | 8 hours | **-87%** |
| Compliance Score | 65% | 98% | **+33 pp** |

### Testimonial

> *"Fused Gaming's systematic approach to standardization, combined with real change management support, transformed how we operate."*  
> — Dr. Michael Wong, President, Central Valley Community College

### Relevance to Peralta
- Direct experience with functional alignment across departments
- Process standardization methodology directly applicable
- Change management approach proven with 150+ staff
- Scalable to Peralta's 4-campus, multi-department environment
