## Cost Assumptions & Constraints

### Baseline Assumptions

1. **Staffing:** 8 full-time equivalent staff as specified in delivery team structure
2. **Labor Market:** Bay Area 2026 market rates for senior technical talent
3. **ERP Systems:** Standard Banner or Colleague ERP; custom APIs supported
4. **Campuses:** 4 campuses (Berkeley, Laney, Merritt, Vista) with standard workflows
5. **Technology Stack:** Cloud-based (AWS/Azure); no legacy on-premise systems
6. **Schedule:** 9-10 month post-award implementation timeline
7. **Contingency:** 10% reserve allocated to top risks

## Cost Efficiency Strategy

Fused Gaming maintains a **17% profit margin** (vs. industry standard 25-30%) to position competitively while maintaining quality:

- **Labor Optimization:** Experienced team reduces rework cycles
- **Methodology:** Proven 7-phase roadmap minimizes surprises
- **Technology:** Claude Flow v3 reduces integration complexity vs. traditional ERP implementations
- **Parallel Execution:** SyncPulse skill enables concurrent workstreams
